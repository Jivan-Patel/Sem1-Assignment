Mumbai

Mumbai, the capital of Maharashtra, is India’s financial, commercial, and entertainment hub. Known as the “City of Dreams,” it’s a place where people from all over the country come to chase opportunities.

The city is home to the Bombay Stock Exchange, Bollywood—the world’s largest film industry—and iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (a UNESCO World Heritage Site).

Mumbai’s fast-paced lifestyle is balanced by its cultural diversity, with festivals, street food, and historic neighborhoods adding to its charm. Despite its hustle and bustle, the city carries an unmatched energy and spirit of resilience, making it one of the most dynamic and influential cities in India.
