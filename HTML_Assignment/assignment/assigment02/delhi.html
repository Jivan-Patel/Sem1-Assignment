Delhi

Delhi is the capital city of India.It is famous for Red Fort, India Gate,and street food at Chandni Chowk.

Delhi a vibrant blend of history, culture, and modernity. Known as the heart of the nation, it has been home to powerful empires and carries a rich heritage visible in its monuments like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate.It is divided into two parts: Old Delhi, famous for its narrow lanes, bustling markets, and Mughal-era charm, and New Delhi, planned by the British with wide roads, government buildings, and modern infrastructure.

Delhi is not just a political hub but also a cultural hotspot, offering diverse cuisines, festivals, and traditions that reflect India’s unity in diversity. Today, Delhi stands as a city where ancient history coexists with rapid urban growth, making it a true symbol of India’s past, present, and future.
